# GIAIC Batch 1 Graduation Requirements

All GIAIC students will complete the following requirements for graduating the program.

## Certification Grades

- **Grade A**: 90% and above
- **Grade B**: 80% - 89%
- **Grade C**: 70% - 79%
- **Fail**: Less than 69%

## Grade Composition

The final certification grades will consist of two parts:

- **Part 1**: Hackathon 1 & 2 = **40%** of your grade (20% each)
- **Part 2**: Hackathon 0 & 5, and Final Exam = **60%** of your grade (20% each)

#### General Agents: Foundations Final Exam

- **Total Questions**: 120
- **Time**: 2 hours 30 minutes

#### Exam Coverage

The final exam will cover the first six chapters of Part 1 of our textbook:

1. [Chapter 1: The AI Agent Factory Paradigm](https://agentfactory.panaversity.org/docs/General-Agents-Foundations/agent-factory-paradigm)
